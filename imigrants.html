About the crisis

The rohingyan reffugee crisis is about the rohingya people an ethnic minority who live in the country of Myanmar. Myanmar mostley a bhuddist country refuses to accept the rohingya people as citizens of there country and there for refuses to give them citizenship to there country. Myanmar sees the rohingya people as illegal immigrants who have come from Neighbouring Country Bangladesh. The rohingyan people are being persecuted and the majority of these people are fleeing to neighbouring countries that are majority muslim to seek a better life.
